ABOUT

"He may bring you happiness!?"

Created by the Japanese toy manufacturer, Toru Soeya, Sonny Angels are cherub figurines that come in a variety of headgear and outfits. They were designed to be a tiny companion for working women in their mid-20s navigating through the stresses of adulthood. Over the summer, I decided to begin a small collection of my own. Currently, I own 17 figurines from multiple series of collections. I'm always looking to meet other collectors who I can share this hobby with, so don't hesitate to reach out!
